The Intelligent Body

I discovered yoga 10 years ago, and it was a revelation. It began as a way to build physical strength, but it quickly became the practice that nurtured my mental clarity and redefined my resilience. That transformation was so powerful that I knew I had to share it.

Now, as a Yoga Alliance ERYT-500 certified instructor with vast experience of mentoring Yoga teacher trainings, my mission is to help you find that same inner peace and power. Whether we are flowing together on the pristine shores of Saadiyat Beach, in a focused studio session, or in the sanctuary of your own home, I lead with one goal: to create an environment where you feel empowered to explore your limits.

I believe in the profound intelligence of the body and the transformative power of breath. My joy comes from guiding you on your personal journey, helping you cultivate a life of growth, mindfulness, and deep, meaningful connection. Let’s discover what your body and mind are truly capable of.

Adel

Founder, ERYT-500

Studio
